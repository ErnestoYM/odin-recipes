Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese is a staple dish in all kinds of dinners! It's easy to make, diverse, and can be absolutely delicious either baked or straight from a pot. To make easy mac and cheese, I will show you the basic instructions for some fast mac and cheese from the pot! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Premade pasta of your choice (we'll use elbow pasta)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheeses of your choice
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Fill a medium-sized pot halfway with water, and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Pour your choice of pasta into pot with some salt and stir for ten minutes.
  3. Once pasta is al dente, drain the pasta into a strainer.
  4. Using the same pot, pour 2tbsp of butter, 1 cup of milk, and as much cheese as you want and stir until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Once the sauce is ready, pour the pasta back in and stir the sauce evenly onto the pasta. Make sure to add in salt and pepper to your liking during this step.
  6. Voila! You've successfully made a good looking pot of mac and cheese! Enjoy it as a dinner for one, family dinner, or Thanksgiving side dish!
