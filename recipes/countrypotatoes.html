Country Potatoes

A good ol' Southern Dish full of love, warmth, and carbs! Learn how to make this delicious breakfast or lunch meal with just a couple of vegetables and time!

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Yellow Onion
  • Variety of Bell Peppers
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Thyme
  • Chives
  • Optional: Cheese and/or Bacon

Instructions

  1. Add 1/4 cup of oil to your skillet and heat over medium heat.
  2. Add 2lbs of diced potatoes, 1 cup of onions, and 1/2 cup (each) of your bell peppers into the skillet. Add the salt, black pepper, and garlic powder to your liking. Toss to evenly coat.
  3. Once coated evenly, cover the skillet tightly and let cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Afterwards, remove the lid and toss it around more. Add your cooked bacon and/or cheese at this point. Continue to cook for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Stir until potatoes are golden-brown and fork tender. Sprinkle in your chopped thyme and chives.
  6. Congrats! You made yourself a good skillet of country potatoes! Enjoy on its own or as a side dish!
